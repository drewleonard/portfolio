I’m Adam Schwartz, lead designer of Cloudflare Apps and freelance film composer. Previously I founded Eager which was acquired by Cloudflare. Prior to that I was a principal software engineer at HubSpot. I’m a huge proponent of open-source software and maintain a number of projects.

I’ve been writing music for over a decade and hope to one day score major motion pictures. I composed the opening for Al Jazeera America’s show Inside Story which aired nightly 2013–2016. I’m currently working on the score for an upcoming indie puzzle platformer videogame.
